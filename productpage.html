The Mainstay FNDTN011 is a universally flattering style that will suit just about any face shape. These D-frame glasses are subtly curved at the browline and along the bottom of the lenses to balance and flatter strong, angled features. This flexible and feather-light pair will sit comfortably on medium faces. Note: due to frame thickness, this style only accommodates prescriptions from -4.00 to +2.50.
