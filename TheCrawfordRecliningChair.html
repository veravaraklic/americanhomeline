The Crawford Reclining Chair

The Crawford Luxury Reclining Club Chair is suited for any king or queen of the house. You have ample of space to kick your feet up and recline. The Crawford Luxury Reclining Chair is perfect for watching TV, reading a book or even a cat nap. Relax in comfort on the overstuffed, billowing cushions. You’re full supported from your head, neck, back and arms. The leg support gives you maximum support from the back of your legs, your calves to your ankles. Great for those who are on their feet all day and who need to take the pressure of their joints. The Crawford is beautifully upholstered in a contemporary, gray chenille fabric. The luxury fabric is upgraded with additional natural fibers. This gives the fabric extra durability and softness. The fabric is pet and child-friendly. The recliner is built on solid a foundation — heavy duty metal reclining mechanism and a frame made from furniture grade woods.
