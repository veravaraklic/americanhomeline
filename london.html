The London Luxury Chenille Collection

The London Luxury Chenille Collection sports clean, contemporary lines. It's juxtaposed with a traditional chenille upholstery — giving the collection warmth and softness.
 
Chenille
Chenille is a French word for catepillar. The soft fibres of the chenille upholstery is woven with stain-resistant polyester yarn and cotton for extra durability. The timeless gray upholstery has tones of black, blues and white, which makes it easy to match the collection with any style of decor.
 
Reinforced Stitching
The London Luxury Collection has reinforced stitching on the cushions and the billowing pillow-top arms — giving it additional strength, in all the areas that get the most wear.

The London Inspiration

The London was inspired by a weekend trip to a friend's cottage. Out in the fresh air all day and cozying up by a fire at night. It was a very warm, casual livingroom where we could settle in and reminisce about our days backpacking across Europe (living off just a baguette and a wedge of cheese). We wanted to create a collection that exudes warmth and comfort. A collection that was going to welcome you as soon as you got home! We used the best of transitional design — marrying both traditional and contemporary elements, giving the London Collection a timeless feel.
