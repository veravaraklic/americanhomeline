Lorraine Bel-Aire Ebony Reclining Loveseat

You’ll never feel too squishy sitting beside someone on the Lorraine Bel-Aire Deluxe Reclining loveseat. Both of you and your friend can stretch out and relax. Recline the seats and kick your feet up in varying positions. First class lounging all the way! The Lorraine has the perfect balance of modern, straight lines with the comforts of billowing back, seat and arm cushions. The cushions are made with high density foam which will retain its shape and comfort over time. It’s upholstered in a beautiful ebony, Bel-Aire leather — which looks and feels like top grain leather without the maintenance. Its breathable leather keeps you cool in the summer and just right in the winter. Underneath it all, it’s made from solid craftsmanship and construction. The wood frame is made with furniture grade wood which will stand the test of use. The recliner is made from high-quality steel which will recline smoothly and strong enough to support you. The Lorraine Bel-Aire Deluxe Ebony Love Seat is so sophisticated, you’d never think that it was a recliner!

Product specs

Loveseat: 57”(w) x 37.4” (d) x 40” (h)
The frame is made from plywood and high-performance furniture grade woods. The combination of woods provides extra durability, stability and strength. This ensures that the frame will not crack, shrink or warp overtime. Stainless steel reclining mechanism. The cushions are made with high density foam.
Bel-Aire leather – finished split hide, natural fibers and polyurethane
