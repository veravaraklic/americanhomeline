The Lorraine Bel-Aire Luxury Reclining Collection

The Lorraine Bel-Aire Luxury Recliner Collection is designed for people who love leather and the comforts of a recliner. The Lorraine is made with Bel-Aire leather — a finished split hide, mixed with other properties such as natural fibres and polyuerthane to give it addtional durablility and a flawless finish.
 
Leather
The Lorraine comes in two leather options, in a mocha (rich, warm brown) or in ebony (midnight black). Both of these colors are timeless and anchors a room. The mocha and the ebony options are both beautiful to look at and even nicer when you're sitting back and relaxing.
 
Reinforced Stitching
The Lorraine Luxury Recliner Collection has added reinforced stitching on the cushions and the billowing pillow-top arms. It adds elements of visual interest, plus strength, in all the areas that get the most wear.

The Lorraine Inspiration

The Lorraine was inspired by a trip we took to England. During our visit, we fell in love with this after-work lounge in Canary Wharf. It was reminiscent of a sophistcated, 19th century English Gentlemen's Club. Where you could relax and have a chat with your mates. We updated the look for the modern urbanite in mind. One who loves the classical elements of rich leathers with the comforts of being able to lounge in style.
