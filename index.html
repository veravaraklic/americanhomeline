American Home Line

American Home Line designs furniture and mattresses that’s inspired by everyday life. All our designs are not just functional, but also beautiful and timeless. We draw inspiration from the world around us — art, people and life.

Each collection is inspired by experiences we want to share with you — the very best in modern/transitional design, that is accessible to everyone. We create pieces that we hope you'll enjoy for a life time. A place to create and share memories.
