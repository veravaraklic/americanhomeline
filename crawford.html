The Crawford Luxury Reclining Collection

The Crawford Luxury Recliner Collection is based on comfort and timeless design. The upholstery is made from the finest fabric, woven with stain-resistant polyester and cotton — for added softness and durability. Chenille offers a nice texture which is soft to the touch and it a gorgeous sheen. The slate colored upholstery has flecks of black and mid-tone grays which make it super contemporary and easy to match any decor.
 
Recliner
The Crawford reclines back easily to suit your favorite position. You can sit with just your feet up or lean all the way back. Which ever your preference, you have full control. The recliner is made from solid-steel which gives you the support, stability and longevity you want! It'll always recline smoothly with ease.
 
Loveseat Storage Console
The storage console features a hidden compartment to store your remotes and your favorite book, while the drink holders can hold up to two drinks at a time. Everything is right at your finger tips.

The Crawford Inspiration

It is inspired by the coziness of home - to give every family member a place to sit back and relax. Its contemporary/transitional design shows off the Crawford's clean, modern lines. It's upholstered in a sophisticated dark gray chenille fabric made from high end fabrics and natural fibres. It's designed to give you the ultimate comfort - with added cushioniong for support in all the right spots plus a manual recliner. When you want to kick your feet up you can!
