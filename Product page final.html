The Crawford Reclining 3 Piece Sofa Set

The Crawford Luxury Recliner Set gives you the best of both worlds. You can recline and put your feet up. When you’re done relaxing, set the recliners to its original upright position.
With the clean, contemporary styling, you’d never know that your three-piece sofa set was a recliner!
For added luxury, the sofa set is upholstered in a ultra-soft, gray chenille fabric. Its silky to the touch and visually it provides a texturized sheen. The luxury edition has been upgraded. The fabric has been woven with additional natural fibers to provide the Crawford Soft Set with extra durability, breathability and softness.
The Crawford is designed with maximum comfort in mind for each individual member of your family. Regardless if you like sitting upright or if your spouse enjoy laying-back you can do that while feeling fully supported.
The billowing back cushions are ergonomically designed to support your head, neck and back. The wide pillow-arms give you plenty of space to rest with ease.
It’s built with solid craftsmanship. The frame is made from furniture grade woods and a heavy-duty reclining mechanism.
The Crawford provides the ultimate in comfort with modern styling that will look great for years to come.

Product specs

Sofa: L 82 “ x D 36“ x H 40“
Loveseat: L 72 “ x D 36“ x H 40“
Chair: L 36" x D 36" x H 40"
The frame is made from plywood and high-performance furniture grade woods. The combination of woods provides extra durability, stability and strength. This ensures that the frame will not crack, shrink or warp overtime.
This reclining set lets you recline in the position that’s most comfortable for you by using the manual latch located on the side of the sofa. (Note that the middle seat of the sofa does not recline).
